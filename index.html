At Maternal Health Monitoring System, we specialize in designing and implementing advanced maternal health monitoring tools. Our system is tailored to identify, track, and manage risks among pregnant and postpartum women. By leveraging vital sign data, symptoms, medical history, and categorization results through our mobile applications, we empower healthcare workers to classify women into low, moderate, or high-risk categories. Our commitment to maternal health ensures that every woman receives the care and attention she deserves, enhancing outcomes for mothers and their children.

Maternal health risk assessment

Our Maternal Health Risk Assessment service utilizes advanced technology to evaluate the health risks of pregnant and postpartum women. By collecting vital signs, symptoms, and medical history through mobile applications, we ensure comprehensive data collection. Our system analyzes this information using a rule-based risk engine to classify women into low, moderate, or high-risk categories based on clinical indicators. This proactive approach enables healthcare providers to tailor interventions and support for women at different risk levels, ensuring a safer maternal health experience.

Mobile data collection Website

Our Mobile Data Collection Website empowers pregnant and postpartum women to easily record their vital signs, symptoms, and medical history. This user-friendly app simplifies the process of documenting essential health information, ensuring accurate and timely data input. The collected data feeds directly into our maternal health monitoring system, enabling healthcare providers to assess risk levels effectively. This innovative technology not only enhances patient engagement but also fosters better communication between patients.

Tailored maternal health management plans

Our Tailored Maternal Health Management Plans are designed to provide personalized care for pregnant and postpartum women based on their assessed risk levels. Using the insights gained from our risk assessment and data collection, healthcare providers can develop specific management plans that address the unique needs of each woman. These plans may include lifestyle recommendations, monitoring schedules, and medical interventions as necessary. By focusing on individualized care, we aim to enhance maternal health outcomes.
